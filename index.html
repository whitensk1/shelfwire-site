About this project

Who I am

A freelance / in-house style advertising agent and content operator focused on one product line at a time. I work with goods that are sold wholesale and need clear visual storytelling on modern platforms.

Who I am not

I am not presenting this site as the brand owner or manufacturer. Brand names of clients stay private. This is a personal professional presence for content, distribution, and automation tools I use in my work.

Catalog storytelling

Wholesale focus

Content is built around wholesale and multi-SKU assortments relevant to buyers and partners in Russia and related export routes. Formats stay practical: clear titles, honest descriptions, and links when a public storefront exists.

  • B2B-oriented product copy in English
  • Visuals suitable for marketplace and social discovery
  • No scraping of third-party accounts
  • Publishing only to channels I am authorized to manage

Publishing tools

To keep posting consistent, I use a private automation stack (Social Poster) that connects to official platform APIs (including Pinterest API v5) with tokens I control. The app is used for my own publishing workflows — not as a public multi-tenant SaaS for random third parties.

When registering developer apps (Pinterest, Meta, etc.), this website is the public project home and the privacy policy lives at /privacy.html.
